About Ebire School

Ebire Creche, Nursery and Primary School is one of the most established schools in Ibadan. For more than five decades, we have been dedicated to shaping confident, intelligent, and morally upright children.

Our approach combines academic excellence, discipline, creativity, and care — ensuring every child receives individual attention in a nurturing environment.

Our Academic Programs

Creche

A safe, loving environment where infants and toddlers are cared for with attention, warmth, and early learning activities.

Nursery School

Primary School

Comprehensive academic curriculum focused on excellence, discipline, confidence, and preparation for secondary education.
